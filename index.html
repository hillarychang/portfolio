About

Hi! My name is Hillary Chang, and I am currently pursuing a bachelor's in Data Science with a minor in Mathematics at the University of California, San Diego. I am interested in software development and AI, and I enjoy exploring different web technologies, coding programs, and constructing devices using microcontrollers.
